MAXIM TOURS

Maxim Tours is an Egyptian tourism company and one of the leading companies in the tourism industry in Egypt. The company offers a wide range of tourism services, including: Organizing international trips to various destinations around the world. Providing religious tourism services, including Hajj and Umrah trips. Offering visa services for multiple countries, such as Turkey, the UAE, Algeria, Latin American countries, the USA, Canada, Australia, East Asian countries, Tunisia, Europe, Morocco, Russia, and Cyprus. Booking airline tickets. Organizing honeymoon trips both within Egypt and abroad. Providing cruise trips in Luxor and Aswan, as well as Mediterranean cruises.
