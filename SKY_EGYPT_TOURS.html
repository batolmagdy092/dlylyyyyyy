SKY EGYPT TOURS

"Sky Egypt Tours" is an Egyptian travel and tourism agency specializing in providing a variety of tourism services. The agency offers various packages, including flight tickets, domestic and international tours, honeymoon trips, and Nile cruises.


It was established in 2000. Headquarters: 8 Omaret El Obour, Salah Salem, Heliopolis, Cairo. Mohandessin Branch: 80 Mohy El Din Abu El Ezz St., in front of Gate 2 of the Hunting Club, Mohandessin, Cairo. New Cairo/Tagamoa Branch: South Teseen Street, Hala Mall 314, Second Floor, next to Cleopatra Ceramics, New Cairo. Contact Numbers: Mohandessin: 01280007500 Tagamoa: 01280007600 Heliopolis: 01280007400 Email: sky@skyeg.com


For more information, you can visit the company's website: https://www.skyegtours.com/


Services Provided: Flight Tickets: The agency offers the cheapest flight ticket prices in Egypt, with the option to book both domestic and international flights. SKYEGTOURS.COM Domestic and International Tours: The agency organizes tours within Egypt to cities such as Hurghada, Marsa Alam, and Sharm El Sheikh, as well as international tours to destinations like Cyprus, the Maldives, and Malaysia. SKYEGTOURS.COM Honeymoon Trips: The agency offers special honeymoon packages, including stays in luxury hotels and resorts both inside and outside Egypt. SKYEGTOURS.COM Nile Cruises: The agency organizes Nile cruises between Aswan and Luxor, offering various sightseeing programs.
