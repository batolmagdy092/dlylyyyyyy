SEEGYPT

"SEEGYPT and the World Travel" is an Egyptian travel and tourism agency specializing in designing custom trips to Egypt, Jordan, and other Middle Eastern countries. The agency offers a variety of programs, including honeymoon trips, student programs, hotel bookings, and tours to destinations such as Sinai, the Red Sea, and Luxor. The agency also provides guide services in English, French, Spanish, Italian, and German


It was established in 1984. The agency's office is located at 32, 87 Street, Maadi, Cairo. You can contact them by phone at the numbers: (202) 3780972, (202) 3784771, (202) 3592634, (202) 3592639, or via email at torg@link.net.


For more information, you can visit the company's website: https://www.seegypt.com.eg/


The agency offers a variety of tourism programs, including: Sinai: Trips to St. Catherine and Dahab (4 days/3 nights), Sharm El Sheikh (4 days/3 nights), Nuweiba and safari in the Sinai Desert (4 days/3 nights), Taba (4 days/3 nights), El Arish and the Bedouin market (4 days/3 nights). Upper Egypt: Nile Cruise (4 days/3 nights), Aswan and Abu Simbel (3 days/2 nights). Western Desert: Safari by 4x4 vehicles and camels in the Western Desert and oases, Siwa Oasis, and Marsa Matruh. Middle East: Trips to Israel (5 days/4 nights), Jordan (5 days/4 nights), Lebanon (5 days/4 nights), Syria (5 days/4 nights).
